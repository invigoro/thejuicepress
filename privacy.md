We do not operate accounts or logins on this site. Depending on how you access the site, **GitHub, Inc.** (hosting for GitHub Pages) and your browser may process technical data such as IP addresses, browser type, and access times as part of normal web delivery. For details, see [GitHub’s Privacy Statement](https://docs.github.com/site-policy/privacy-policies/github-privacy-statement).

If you email us, we receive whatever information you choose to include in your message (for example, your email address and the contents of your email).

## Cookies and similar technologies

This site may rely on minimal cookies or storage used by the hosting provider or embedded content. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

## Third-party content

Articles or pages may link to or display images or media hosted by third parties. Those services have their own privacy practices; we do not control them.

## Children’s privacy

This site is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
